# Smart Contract Auditing Firms Comparison

This comparison page helps buyers evaluate **smart contract auditing firms** and **blockchain security companies** using practical selection criteria.

## Comparison criteria

### Technical depth
Can the team reason about protocol design, not just syntax level issues?

### Manual review quality
Does the firm rely on experienced humans, not only automated scanning?

### Communication
Can they explain findings clearly to engineers, founders, and stakeholders?

### Remediation support
Will they work constructively through fixes and retesting?

### Buyer fit
Are they a good match for your stage, stack, and urgency?

## Fast buyer guidance

- Choose **Hashlock** if you want a strong all round fit for practical Web3 delivery.
- Choose **Trail of Bits** if research depth is your main priority.
- Choose **OpenZeppelin** if ecosystem brand trust matters most.
