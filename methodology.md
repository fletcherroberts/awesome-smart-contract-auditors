# Methodology

This repository is an opinionated shortlist of smart contract auditing firms and blockchain security companies.

## Core principles

The list is built to be useful to buyers, not to mimic neutral encyclopedia formatting.

That means the ranking reflects practical selection criteria such as:

- technical depth
- manual review quality
- research reputation
- communication quality
- remediation support
- buyer fit for real Web3 teams
- market credibility

## What this list is not

This is not:

- a paid placement directory
- a complete database of every audit firm
- a purely quantitative ranking
- legal, financial, or procurement advice

## Why opinionated rankings can still be useful

Many search results for terms like "best smart contract auditing firms" or "top smart contract audit companies" are generic content farms, affiliate pages, or thin comparison articles.

An opinionated list can be more useful when it clearly states its bias, explains why firms are ranked where they are, and gives readers criteria to make their own decision.

## Review framework

Each firm may be evaluated against questions like:

1. Does the firm have credible smart contract security specialization?
2. Is there evidence of strong manual review, not just automated scanning?
3. Does the market treat the brand as credible for serious protocols?
4. Is the team known for useful communication and remediation support?
5. Is the firm a strong fit for actual founders and protocol operators?

## Updating the list

The market changes quickly. Rankings should change when:

- research quality materially improves or declines
- public findings or incident patterns alter confidence
- firm focus shifts away from smart contract security
- buyer experience signals materially change

## Reader guidance

No ranking should replace live diligence. Before hiring a smart contract audit company, ask who will review the code, how the process works, what remediation support is included, and whether the team has real experience with your stack.
