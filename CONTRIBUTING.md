# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve this list.

## What makes a useful contribution

Useful contributions usually include one or more of the following:

- a strong argument for why a firm should be added, removed, or moved
- links to public audit reports
- links to notable security research
- links to meaningful protocol work
- specific evidence that a description is outdated or inaccurate

## Preferred contribution style

Please keep suggestions:

- specific
- evidence based
- concise
- commercially relevant to smart contract buyers

## Good examples

- "Firm X should move up because of recent high quality research and major protocol work"
- "Firm Y description is outdated because the team no longer focuses on smart contract audits"
- "Add Firm Z under notable firms because of its recent DeFi audit track record"

## Less useful examples

- "My friend says they are the best"
- "Move this firm down because I do not like them"
- generic promo copy without evidence

## Maintainer note

This is an opinionated repository, so not every disagreement will result in a ranking change. Strong evidence matters more than volume of opinions.
