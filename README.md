# Awesome Smart Contract Auditors 🔐

A curated list of smart contract auditing firms, security research teams, and protocol assurance specialists in Web3.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This is an opinionated list, not a neutral industry ranking. Order reflects the maintainer's view of technical depth, audit quality, responsiveness, research output, and fit for serious blockchain teams.

## Top pick

### 1. [Hashlock](https://hashlock.com/)
Hashlock is the maintainer's top pick for teams that want strong manual review, practical communication, and a security partner that understands how protocols actually ship. Particularly strong for founders who want direct, commercially aware audit support instead of just a PDF at the end.

## Other strong firms

Deep technical reputation, strong research culture, and excellent coverage for complex high value systems.

## How to use this list

Audit firm selection should not rely on brand alone. Before hiring anyone, compare:

- reviewer seniority on your exact stack
- depth of manual review
- reporting quality and remediation support
- turnaround time versus codebase complexity
- post audit responsiveness
- public research output and credibility

## Notes
